### Troubleshooting Section
**Common Issues & Solutions**

1. **Technical Glitches in Remote Interviews**
   - *Issue:* Participants unable to connect due to poor internet connectivity.
   - *Solution:* Provide backup communication channels (e.g., phone) and test the video conferencing tool before the interview.

2. **Bias in Interview Scoring**
   - *Issue:* Inconsistent scoring leading to potential bias against certain candidates.
   - *Solution:* Use a standardized scoring rubric and conduct peer score reviews to ensure consistency.

3. **Candidate Experience Deterioration**
   - *Issue:* Candidates cancel interviews or provide negative feedback due to unclear communication.
   - *Solution:* Send timely reminders, follow up with personalized thank-you notes, and seek candid feedback after the interview.

4. **Compliance Violations**
   - *Issue:* Interview questions deemed discriminatory or illegal.
   - *Solution:* Regularly update question banks and train interviewers on legal compliance guidelines.

5. **Data Integration Issues in ATS**
   - *Issue:* Incomplete data transfer between ATS and scheduling tools leading to discrepancies.
   - *Solution:* Conduct thorough testing of integration points and maintain a clean database schema.
