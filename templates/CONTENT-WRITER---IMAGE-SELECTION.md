# Content Writer - AI Agent Template
## Image Selection

### Success Definition (Measurable)
**Success Criteria:**
- **Primary Metric:** 90%+ of images selected are relevant to content theme.
- **Secondary Metrics:**
  - 85% or higher average user satisfaction rating from image selection process.
  - Zero copyright infringement incidents reported in the first quarter post-selection.
  - Reduction in editing time per image by at least 20% compared to baseline.

**Timeline:** Achieve full implementation and meet all metrics within 30 days of starting the workflow.

### Execution Steps (Detailed)

**STEP 1: Content Theme Definition**
- **Action:** Define the main themes and concepts of the upcoming content series.
- **Tools Needed:** Google Docs or Notion for outlining themes.
- **Success Criteria:** Documented list of 3-5 key themes with brief descriptions.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overly broad themes that lack focus.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 days

**STEP 2: Keyword Research**
- **Action:** Use tools like Google Trends and SEMrush to find relevant keywords for the images.
- **Tools Needed:** [Google Trends, SEMrush, Ahrefs]
- **Success Criteria:** List of top 10 relevant keywords with search volume data.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking long-tail keywords or using outdated trends.
- **Time Estimate:** 3 days

**STEP 3: Image Licensing Check**
- **Action:** Verify that all potential images are licensed for commercial use and free from copyright restrictions.
- **Tools Needed:** [Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay]
- **Success Criteria:** All selected images are verified as royalty-free or commercially usable.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Selecting images with restrictive licenses or using stock photos without proper attribution.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 day

**STEP 4: Image Sourcing**
- **Action:** Search for images based on the keywords identified, ensuring they align with the content theme and licensing requirements.
- **Tools Needed:** [Google Images, Pinterest, DALL-E]
- **Success Criteria:** At least 10 high-quality image options that meet all criteria.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Selecting low-resolution or irrelevant images.
- **Time Estimate:** 4 days

**STEP 5: Image Selection**
- **Action:** Use AI tools to refine the selection based on relevance, quality, and alignment with SEO best practices.
- **Tools Needed:** [DALL-E, Midjourney]
- **Success Criteria:** Final list of 3-5 images that best fit each content piece.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Over-reliance on AI without human review.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 days

**STEP 6: Image Editing**
- **Action:** Use Photoshop or free alternatives like GIMP to edit the selected images for optimal presentation and SEO compliance.
- **Tools Needed:** [Photoshop, GIMP, Canva]
- **Success Criteria:** Images are optimized in terms of size, format, and alt text.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Over-editing leading to loss of quality or mismatched branding.
- **Time Estimate:** 3 days per image

**STEP 7: Image Integration**
- **Action:** Integrate the selected images into the content creation workflow, ensuring they are properly credited and tagged.
- **Tools Needed:** Content management system (CMS) like WordPress or Squarespace.
- **Success Criteria:** Images appear correctly in all content formats with proper SEO tags.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Incorrect file paths or missing alt text.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 day

**STEP 8: Quality Review**
- **Action:** Conduct a thorough review of the selected images to ensure they meet visual, branding, and accessibility standards.
- **Tools Needed:** [Screening tool, Accessibility checker]
- **Success Criteria:** All images pass quality checks with zero errors.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Skipping steps due to time constraints.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 day

**STEP 9: User Feedback Collection**
- **Action:** Share the content with a small group of users for feedback on image relevance and overall impact.
- **Tools Needed:** Google Forms or SurveyMonkey for feedback collection.
- **Success Criteria:** At least 5 pieces of constructive feedback received and implemented.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Gathering non-specific or negative feedback without actionable insights.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 10: Final Approval**
- **Action:** Obtain final approval from the client or stakeholder on all selected images before publication.
- **Tools Needed:** [Email, Document sharing platforms]
- **Success Criteria:** Signed-off document with no outstanding changes.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Missed approvals leading to last-minute revisions.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 day

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

1. **Image Licensing Issues:**
   - Verify Creative Commons licenses carefully.
   - Contact the image provider if unsure.

2. **Content Inconsistency:**
   - Ensure images align with brand guidelines (color palette, style).
   - Use a visual hierarchy check.

3. **SEO Compliance Problems:**
   - Check alt text and file names for keywords.
   - Avoid using "nofilter" or similar non-informative tags.

4. **Editing Quality Loss:**
   - Keep original high-resolution files before exporting.
   - Test image compression tools to avoid quality degradation.

5. **Version Control Issues:**
   - Use a naming convention that includes version number and date.
   - Store all versions in a dedicated folder structure.

### Actionable Guidance for Beginners

1. **Start Small:** Begin with one piece of content to practice the workflow.
2. **Focus on Quality Over Quantity:** Choose a few high-quality images rather than many mediocre ones.
3. **Use Free Resources First:** Leverage Unsplash, Pexels, and Pixabay before investing in premium tools.
4. **Document Everything:** Keep detailed records of sources, licensing, and changes made for future reference.
5. **Iterate Quickly:** Use the feedback loop to refine images quickly without getting stuck.
