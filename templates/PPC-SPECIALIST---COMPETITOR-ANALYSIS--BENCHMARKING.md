## PHASE 3: EXECUTION WORKFLOW

### Step-by-Step Process

**STEP 1: [Competitive Landscape Mapping]**
- **Action:** Use SEMrush or Ahrefs to map out top competitors' PPC strategies.
- **Tools Needed:** SEMrush/Ahrefs (free trial available), Google Sheets for data visualization.
- **Success Criteria:** Comprehensive map of competitor campaigns, keywords, and ad placements across 3 main platforms (Google Search/Display, Facebook).
- **Common Pitfalls:** Missing hidden or new ad groups; Over-reliance on outdated tools.
- **Time Estimate:** 5 days

**STEP 2: [Keyword Gap Analysis]**
- **Action:** Perform a keyword gap analysis to identify keywords competitors are targeting but your business isn't.
- **Tools Needed:** SEMrush, Ahrefs Keyword Overview.
- **Success Criteria:** List of at least 20 high-value gaps with estimated search volume and competition level.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Ignoring long-tail opportunities; Overlooking low-competition terms that could be valuable.
- **Time Estimate:** 3 days

**STEP 3: [Ad Copy Benchmarking]**
- **Action:** Analyze ad copy across competitors using a combination of screenshots and AI-driven text analysis tools (e.g., Grammarly).
- **Tools Needed:** Browser extensions for screenshot capture, Google Docs for comparative review.
- **Success Criteria:** At least 10 headlines optimized for click-through rates with justification based on industry benchmarks.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Copy similarity leading to creative fatigue; Ignoring cultural relevance in localized markets.
- **Time Estimate:** 4 days

**STEP 4: [Landing Page Performance Review]**
- **Action:** Conduct a site audit using GTmetrix and WebPageTest for top landing pages across competitors.
- **Tools Needed:** GTmetrix, WebPageTest (free tier available).
- **Success Criteria:** Identification of at least 3 technical issues affecting performance; Recommendations to improve load time by X%.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Focusing only on visual design elements; Ignoring accessibility and SEO factors.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 days

**STEP 5: [Budget Allocation Optimization]**
- **Action:** Use a budget modeling tool (e.g., Google's Budget Simulation) to allocate spend across ad groups based on historical performance data.
- **Tools Needed:** Google Ads Budget Simulation, Excel for financial modeling.
- **Success Criteria:** Revised allocation plan showing expected ROI improvement of at least 10% with current keyword sets.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Over-allocation to high CPC keywords without conversion analysis; Ignoring seasonal trends.
- **Time Estimate:** 3 days

**STEP 6: [A/B Testing Strategy Implementation]**
- **Action:** Set up A/B tests for ad copy, landing pages, and audience targeting using Google Ads' built-in testing tools.
- **Tools Needed:** Google Ads Experiments, Optimizely or VWO for landing page testing.
- **Success Criteria:** At least 5 active experiments with planned duration of 2 weeks each; Statistical significance achieved (>95% confidence).
- **Common Pitfalls:** Insufficient sample size leading to inconclusive results; Testing too many variables simultaneously.
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing (start within week)

**STEP 7: [Monitoring & Reporting Setup]**
- **Action:** Create a dashboard using Google Data Studio or Tableau Public to monitor key metrics daily/weekly.
- **Tools Needed:** Google Data Studio, Tableau Online (free tier), Excel for historical comparison.
- **Success Criteria:** Dashboard reflects real-time performance against targets; Alerts configured for critical drops in CTR/CPC.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Inconsistent data updates leading to stale insights; Overloading dashboard with too many metrics.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 days

**STEP 8: [Continuous Optimization Loop]**
- **Action:** Schedule a weekly review meeting to analyze performance, adjust bids, and refresh creative assets.
- **Tools Needed:** Calendar invites for recurring meetings, shared document for action items.
- **Success Criteria:** At least one optimization change implemented each week; Metrics showing positive trend over 4 weeks.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Meeting fatigue leading to missed opportunities; Lack of documentation causing knowledge loss.
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing

## PHASE 4: OPTIMIZATION & REFINEMENT

### Performance Metrics
1. **Primary Metric:** CTR Improvement
   - Target: Achieve at least a **15% increase** in average CTR compared to baseline for all campaigns within the first month.
   - Measurement Method: SEMrush/Google Ads dashboard export and compare against historical averages.

2. **Secondary Metrics:**
   - CPC Reduction: Reduce average CPC by 10% over three months without sacrificing quality score.
   - Conversion Rate Boost: Increase conversion rate by at least **5%** for primary landing pages within two months.
   - Budget Utilization: Maintain a budget utilization rate above **80%** of allocated spend.

3. **Long-term Metrics:**
   - Revenue Growth: Achieve **20% increase** in revenue from PPC campaigns over the first quarter.
   - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Reduce CAC by **10%** within six months through improved targeting and optimization.

### Iterative Improvement Loop
1. Measure current performance against targets.
2. Identify top 3 improvement opportunities based on metrics variance.
3. Implement changes (e.g., adjust bids, refresh ad copy).
4. Re-measure after one cycle to confirm improvements.
5. Document all iterations in the master project log.
