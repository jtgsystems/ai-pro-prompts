# Product Manager - AI Agent Template
## Market Research

### Success Definition:
**Success:** Achieve a 20% increase in market share within 12 months through validated product features derived from in-depth competitor analysis, customer insights, and emerging trends identified via market research.

**Measurable Criteria:**
- **Market Share Growth:** Target +20% YoY
- **Customer Acquisition Rate:** Minimum 15% increase in new user sign-ups over 6 months
- **Product Adoption Rate:** Achieve >30% adoption rate for key product features within the first quarter post-launch
- **Competitive Benchmarking Score:** Outperform top competitors by at least 10 points in customer satisfaction and feature relevance

### Critical Knowledge Areas (12 Topics)

1. **Market Segmentation & Target Identification**
   - Research Focus: Define distinct market segments, prioritize target audience based on demographics, psychographics, buying behavior.
   - Tools: Google Analytics, SurveyMonkey for surveys, CRM systems.

2. **Competitive Landscape Analysis**
   - Research Focus: Identify key competitors, analyze their product offerings, pricing strategies, market positioning.
   - Tools: SEMrush, Ahrefs (free alternatives include Moz), SimilarWeb, Product Hunt.

3. **Customer Journey Mapping**
   - Research Focus: Map out user journeys from awareness to purchase and beyond, identifying pain points and opportunities for engagement.
   - Tools: Miro, Lucidchart (free alternatives), HubSpot CRM.

4. **Voice of the Customer (VoC) Analysis**
   - Research Focus: Analyze customer feedback from reviews, surveys, social media, support tickets.
   - Tools: G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, Sentiment analysis tools like MonkeyLearn.

5. **Market Trend Analysis**
   - Research Focus: Identify emerging trends in the market relevant to product direction and innovation.
   - Tools: Google Trends, TrendHunter (free alternatives include Dateteller).

6. **Regulatory Compliance Review**
   - Research Focus: Ensure understanding of industry regulations affecting product development and marketing.
   - Tools: Legal databases like Westlaw (alternatives are free legal research tools like LawInfo).

7. **Product Positioning & Messaging**
   - Research Focus: Develop messaging that resonates with target segments based on market insights.
   - Tools: Brandwatch, Talkwalker.

8. **Demand Forecasting Techniques**
   - Research Focus: Use statistical methods to predict future demand for product features or the entire product line.
   - Tools: Python (Pandas, NumPy), Excel Power Query (alternatives include R with caret package).

9. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Calculation**
   - Research Focus: Model CLV based on customer acquisition costs and expected usage/revenue patterns.
   - Tools: Cohort analysis tools in Google Data Studio or Looker.

10. **SWOT Analysis Implementation**
    - Research Focus: Conduct a comprehensive SWOT analysis of the product considering market conditions, competition, internal capabilities, and risks.
    - Tools: Excel, Miro (alternatives include free templates on Canva).

11. **Influencer & Community Mapping**
    - Research Focus: Identify key influencers in the industry who can provide insights or endorsements for the product.
    - Tools: BuzzSumo, LinkedIn Influencer Insights.

12. **Pricing Strategy Validation**
    - Research Focus: Validate pricing strategy through competitor analysis and customer willingness-to-pay studies.
    - Tools: Pricing Radar, Competitor Price Tracker (alternatives include manual spreadsheet analysis).

### Execution Workflow

**STEP 1: Market Segmentation & Target Identification**
- **Action:** Conduct primary research using surveys and secondary data from industry reports to define target segments.
- **Tools Needed:** SurveyMonkey, Google Trends
- **Success Criteria:** Identify at least three distinct market segments with clear criteria for inclusion.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Over-reliance on single data source leading to skewed results.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 weeks

**STEP 2: Competitive Landscape Analysis**
- **Action:** Compile a list of competitors, analyze their products, pricing, and marketing strategies using SEMrush and Ahrefs data.
- **Tools Needed:** SEMrush (free alternative - SimilarWeb), Excel
- **Success Criteria:** Create a detailed competitive matrix highlighting strengths and weaknesses relative to our product.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Focusing too much on high-profile competitors while ignoring emerging players.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 3: Customer Journey Mapping**
- **Action:** Use Miro or Lucidchart to map out the customer journey from initial awareness through purchase and post-purchase advocacy.
- **Tools Needed:** Miro (free alternative - Figma)
- **Success Criteria:** Map includes at least five key touchpoints with identified pain points addressed in our product roadmap.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking long-tail stages of the journey like renewal or churn prevention.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 4: Voice of the Customer (VoC) Analysis**
- **Action:** Collect and analyze customer feedback from multiple sources including G2, Capterra, social media sentiment analysis using MonkeyLearn.
- **Tools Needed:** MonkeyLearn, Google Forms
- **Success Criteria:** Develop a prioritized list of top five product improvement suggestions based on frequency and impact potential.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Ignoring negative feedback due to its volume or perceived negativity.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 5: Market Trend Analysis**
- **Action:** Use Google Trends and TrendHunter dashboards to identify emerging trends impacting our market.
- **Tools Needed:** Google Trends, TrendHunter
- **Success Criteria:** Document at least three actionable insights derived from identified trends that inform product development priorities.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Focusing on short-term fads rather than long-term sustainable trends.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 6: Regulatory Compliance Review**
- **Action:** Compile a list of relevant regulations affecting our industry and evaluate their impact on our business model.
- **Tools Needed:** Legal databases, compliance checklists (alternatives include free guides from government agencies).
- **Success Criteria:** No regulatory blockers identified for planned product features or marketing campaigns.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking local variations in regulation that apply to specific regions where we operate.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 7: Product Positioning & Messaging**
- **Action:** Conduct workshops with stakeholders to refine messaging around key features and benefits based on customer insights.
- **Tools Needed:** Brandwatch, Talkwalker
- **Success Criteria:** Develop a clear value proposition that resonates with target segments in at least three different market contexts.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Misalignment between technical capabilities and perceived benefits by potential customers.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 8: Demand Forecasting Techniques**
- **Action:** Apply statistical models like linear regression to historical sales data to predict future demand for key features or products.
- **Tools Needed:** Python (Pandas, NumPy), Excel Power Query
- **Success Criteria:** Predicted demand aligns within Â±10% of actual sales in a pilot test market during the first quarter post-launch.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Using outdated data or failing to account for seasonal factors affecting demand.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 weeks

**STEP 9: Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Calculation**
- **Action:** Model CLV using cohort analysis based on acquisition costs, average usage frequency, and expected revenue per user.
- **Tools Needed:** Google Data Studio, Looker
- **Success Criteria:** Calculate a projected CLV that exceeds customer acquisition cost by at least 20%.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Assuming uniform usage patterns across all segments without segment-specific adjustments.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 10: SWOT Analysis Implementation**
- **Action:** Conduct a structured SWOT analysis using insights from previous steps to identify internal strengths and weaknesses relative to external opportunities and threats.
- **Tools Needed:** Excel, Miro
- **Success Criteria:** Completed SWOT matrix with actionable items prioritized by impact and feasibility.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Focusing too heavily on either opportunities or threats without balancing both equally.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 11: Influencer & Community Mapping**
- **Action:** Identify key influencers in the industry who can provide feedback or endorsements for our product using BuzzSumo and LinkedIn.
- **Tools Needed:** BuzzSumo, LinkedIn
- **Success Criteria:** At least five influential contacts engaged with our content, providing positive feedback or suggestions for improvement.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking niche communities that may offer valuable insights but have smaller reach.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 12: Pricing Strategy Validation**
- **Action:** Validate pricing strategy through surveys and willingness-to-pay studies using SurveyMonkey and A/B testing tools.
- **Tools Needed:** SurveyMonkey, Optimizely
- **Success Criteria:** Achieve a price point that maximizes revenue while maintaining customer satisfaction above 80%.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Failing to account for competitor pricing differentials leading to underpricing or overpricing the product.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

### Troubleshooting Section

**Common Issues & Solutions**

1. **Data Inconsistency**
   - *Issue:* Discrepancies in data from multiple sources (e.g., surveys, social media).
   - *Solution:* Validate data using cross-checking against another source or by calculating averages across datasets.

2. **Tool Limitations**
   - *Issue:* Free tools lack advanced features needed for comprehensive analysis.
   - *Solution:* Upgrade to paid versions of the tool if necessary (e.g., SEMrush premium).

3. **Overlooking Market Changes**
   - *Issue:* Rapid shifts in market dynamics not captured in initial research.
   - *Solution:* Set up alerts for industry-specific keywords and trends.

4. **Feedback Curation Challenges**
   - *Issue:* Too much feedback to analyze manually, leading to missed insights.
   - *Solution:* Use sentiment analysis tools or AI-powered content aggregation platforms.

5. **Regulatory Changes**
   - *Issue:* New regulations impacting the market that were not anticipated.
   - *Solution:* Subscribe to newsletters from regulatory bodies and industry associations.

### Conclusion for Beginners
For someone new to the role of a Product Manager focused on Market Research, start by familiarizing yourself with free tools like Google Trends, SurveyMonkey, and SEMrush Free Version. Begin by defining your target market segments and conducting basic competitive analysis using publicly available data. Gradually build out your research capabilities by incorporating customer feedback through surveys and social listening platforms. As you gain confidence in the process, invest in more advanced analytics tools to refine your insights further.
