## PHASE 3: EXECUTION WORKFLOW

### Step-by-Step Process

**STEP 1: [Legal Document Repository Setup]**
- **Action:** Set up a centralized digital repository using a Legal DMS (e.g., Clio, MyCase).
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - [Clio Manage (free trial)](https://www.clio.com/) or [MyCase](https://mycase.com/)
  - [Google Drive/OneDrive for cloud storage]
- **Success Criteria:** All document types are uploaded and indexed.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Incomplete indexing, lack of access controls.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 hours.

**STEP 2: [Deadlines Entry & Synchronization]**
- **Action:** Enter all jurisdictional deadlines into the DMS.
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - Spreadsheet for manual entry (Google Sheets).
  - API integration with DMS for automated updates.
- **Success Criteria:** Deadlines are synchronized across devices and accessible by authorized users.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Manual data entry errors, lack of synchronization.
- **Time Estimate:** 4 hours.

**STEP 3: [Automated Alerts Setup]**
- **Action:** Configure automated alerts for upcoming deadlines via the DMS or a separate tool like [Asana](https://asana.com/).
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - [Asana (free)](https://asana.com/)
  - Calendar integration for email notifications.
- **Success Criteria:** Users receive timely reminders for all pending deadlines.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Alerts not configured correctly, notification settings disabled.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 hour.

**STEP 4: [Collaboration and Task Assignment]**
- **Action:** Assign tasks related to each document deadline to the appropriate team members.
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - DMS task assignment feature.
  - Collaboration platform like [Slack](https://slack.com/) or [Teams](https://www.office.com/apps/teams).
- **Success Criteria:** All stakeholders have clear, assigned responsibilities for deadlines.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlapping tasks, unclear ownership.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 hours.

**STEP 5: [Client Communication Protocol Setup]**
- **Action:** Establish a protocol for notifying clients about document deadlines and status updates.
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - Email templates in DMS or email client (e.g., Outlook).
  - Client portal access for uploading documents before deadline.
- **Success Criteria:** Clients receive timely notifications about their specific deadlines.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Lack of communication plan, missed client notifications.
- **Time Estimate:** 3 hours.

**STEP 6: [Review and Approval Workflow]**
- **Action:** Implement a review and approval process for documents before they are filed or served.
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - Document control feature in DMS.
  - Version history tracking.
- **Success Criteria:** All documents undergo necessary reviews/approvals on time.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Incomplete reviews, approvals delayed.
- **Time Estimate:** 2 hours.

**STEP 7: [Audit and Compliance Check]**
- **Action:** Regularly audit the Deadline Calendar for compliance with legal standards.
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - Audit report feature in DMS.
  - Compliance checklist template.
- **Success Criteria:** All deadlines are compliant, no pending non-compliance issues.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Lack of regular audits, missing compliance checks.
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing.

**STEP 8: [Reporting and Metrics Tracking]**
- **Action:** Generate reports on deadline adherence and update the master Deadline Calendar.
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - Reporting feature in DMS.
  - Spreadsheet for tracking metrics.
- **Success Criteria:** Reports accurately reflect deadline status and compliance.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Inaccurate data, lack of regular reporting.
- **Time Estimate:** Weekly.

**STEP 9: [Update Maintenance Plan]**
- **Action:** Establish a plan to maintain the Deadline Calendar and update it as needed.
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - Calendar sync across devices (Google Calendar).
  - Regular review schedule in project management tool.
- **Success Criteria:** Calendar is regularly updated, all parties aware of updates.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Calendar not synced, no scheduled reviews.
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing.

**STEP 10: [Disaster Recovery and Backup Plan]**
- **Action:** Implement a backup plan for the Deadline Calendar in case of system failure or data loss.
- **Tools Needed:**  
  - Cloud storage with automatic backups (Google Drive).
  - Offsite storage solutions like external hard drives.
- **Success Criteria:** All deadline data is recoverable in case of disaster.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Lack of backups, no recovery plan.
- **Time Estimate:** Initial setup; ongoing maintenance.

   - Measurement Method: Compare actual deadline dates against recorded dates in DMS.

2. **Secondary Metrics:**

   - [ ] User satisfaction with the Deadline Calendar system.

3. **Long-term Metrics:**
   - [ ] Reduction in document processing time over 6 months.
   - [ ] Increase in client satisfaction ratings related to deadline compliance.

2. Identify top 3 improvement opportunities (e.g., missed deadlines, alert issues).
3. Implement changes (e.g., refine alerts, adjust task assignments).
4. Re-measure and document improvements.
