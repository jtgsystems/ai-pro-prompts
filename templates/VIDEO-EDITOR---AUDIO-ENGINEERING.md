### PHASE 3: EXECUTION WORKFLOW

#### Step-by-Step Process

**STEP 1: Audio Source Preparation**
- **Action:** Organize and label all audio files, ensuring high-quality recordings are prioritized.
- **Tools Needed:** Adobe Audition (free), Audacity (free)
- **Success Criteria:** All source files are organized in a clear directory structure with metadata tags for easy access.

**STEP 2: Initial Mixing Setup**
- **Action:** Import project into chosen DAW, create separate tracks for dialogue, SFX, and music.
- **Tools Needed:** Adobe Premiere Pro (free), DaVinci Resolve (free)
- **Success Criteria:** Tracks are clearly labeled and separated from video layers.

**STEP 3: Dialogue Editing**
- **Action:** Edit out unwanted sections of recorded dialogue, ensuring clean takes are used for final cuts.
- **Tools Needed:** Audition's noise reduction tools
- **Success Criteria:** Clean, intelligible dialogue tracks with minimal background noise.

**STEP 4: Sound Effects Integration**
- **Action:** Place SFX in the appropriate locations within the scene and adjust levels for realism.
- **Tools Needed:** Mix Console, EQ tools
- **Success Criteria:** SFX enhance storytelling without overpowering other audio elements.

**STEP 5: Music Layering**
- **Action:** Add background music tracks, ensuring they complement the visual content without competing with dialogue.
- **Tools Needed:** Crossfade tools
- **Success Criteria:** Music is evenly blended and enhances emotional impact of scenes.

**STEP 6: Dynamic Range Compression**
- **Action:** Apply compression to maintain consistent volume levels across takes and scenes.
- **Tools Needed:** Compressor plugin in DAW
- **Success Criteria:** Audio tracks have stable loudness without clipping or distortion.

**STEP 7: Spatial Audio Configuration (Optional)**
- **Action:** Implement binaural audio for immersive experience, if required by project specifications.
- **Tools Needed:** Binaural recording software
- **Success Criteria:** Soundstage feels three-dimensional and spatial cues are preserved.

**STEP 8: Final Mix Review**
- **Action:** Conduct a full review of the mix in all playback systems (headphones, studio monitors).
- **Tools Needed:** Headphone analysis tools
- **Success Criteria:** Consistent audio quality across formats with no unexpected artifacts.

**STEP 9: Audio Mastering**
- **Action:** Apply final mastering techniques to ensure optimal distribution quality.
- **Tools Needed:** Audacity's mastering plugins, Adobe Premiere's master effects
- **Success Criteria:** Exported files meet target platform specifications and sound professional on all playback systems.

**STEPS 10+:** [Define additional steps based on project complexity]
- Implement AI-driven noise reduction using WaveNet (free)
- Apply dynamic range compression with spectral analysis tools
