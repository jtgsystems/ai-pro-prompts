## PHASE 3: EXECUTION WORKFLOW

### Step-by-Step Process

**STEP 1: [Product Optimization]**
- **Action:** Optimize product titles, bullet points, and descriptions using SEO tools (e.g., Helium 10).
- **Tools Needed:** Helium 10, Ahrefs, SEMrush.
- **Success Criteria:** Titles and descriptions rank in top 3 for relevant keywords.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overstuffing keywords, neglecting mobile optimization.
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week.

**STEP 2: [Email Review Requests]**
- **Action:** Use email automation tools (e.g., Mailchimp) to send review requests after purchase.
- **Tools Needed:** Mailchimp, Klaviyo.
- **Success Criteria:** At least 50% of customers who receive the request leave a review within 7 days.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Sending too many emails, poor subject lines.
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing campaign.

**STEP 3: [Social Media & Influencer Engagement]**
- **Action:** Collaborate with influencers to promote products and encourage reviews.
- **Tools Needed:** Hootsuite for social media scheduling, Upfluence for influencer discovery.
- **Success Criteria:** At least 10 reviews from the collaborative posts within a month.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Low engagement from chosen influencers.
- **Time Estimate:** Monthly campaigns.

**STEP 4: [Review Boost Ads]**
- **Action:** Use Amazon’s Sponsored Highlight feature to promote top products and encourage reviews.
- **Tools Needed:** Amazon Advertising Console.
- **Success Criteria:** Increased visibility with a 20% boost in review requests.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overbidding leading to high costs per click.
- **Time Estimate:** Weekly ad management.

**STEP 5: [Customer Feedback Loop]**
- **Action:** Implement automated responses and thank-you emails for leaving reviews.
- **Tools Needed:** Zapier for automating workflows, Amazon Review Management Tools.
- **Success Criteria:** High response rate to reviewers (e.g., 80%).
- **Common Pitfalls:** Delayed responses leading to lower customer satisfaction.
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing.

**STEPS 6-10:**
1. **Competitor Analysis:** Use tools like Jungle Scout to monitor top products and their review patterns.
2. **Feedback Utilization:** Incorporate positive reviews in marketing materials.
3. **Review Retention:** Follow up with customers who reviewed negatively to improve product or service.
4. **Analytics Setup:** Set up dashboards using Google Data Studio for real-time review metrics.

### Quality Checkpoints
- **Checkpoint 1 (After STEP 2):** Verify email deliverability and open rates are at least 70%.
- **Checkpoint 3 (After STEP 6):** Ensure competitor analysis is updated monthly.
- **Checkpoint 4 (After STEP 8):** Confirm all automated responses are set up correctly.
