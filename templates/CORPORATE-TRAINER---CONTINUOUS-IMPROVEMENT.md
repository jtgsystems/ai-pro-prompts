## PHASE 3: EXECUTION WORKFLOW

### Step-by-Step Process

**STEP 1: [Foundation Setup]**
- **Action:** Develop a comprehensive training framework incorporating the latest instructional design frameworks (e.g., SAM) and aligning with corporate objectives.
- **Tools Needed:** Miro or Lucidchart for visual planning, Google Docs for document management.
- **Success Criteria:** Framework approved by department heads within 2 weeks.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking stakeholder buy-in; mismatch between training goals and business outcomes.
- **Time Estimate:** 4 weeks

**STEP 2: [Initial Implementation]**
- **Action:** Deploy the first phase of the corporate training program using a selected LMS platform, ensuring all content is accessible and optimized for mobile devices.
- **Tools Needed:** Moodle (free), Google Workspace for collaboration; Adobe Captivate or Articulate Storyline for e-learning modules.
- **Success Criteria:** 80% of trainees complete their first module within the first month.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Platform accessibility issues, lack of mobile optimization leading to low completion rates.
- **Time Estimate:** 6 weeks

**STEP 3: [Core Work]**
- **Action:** Develop and integrate microlearning modules focused on high-priority competencies identified in the learning objectives section. Utilize gamification elements where applicable.
- **Tools Needed:** Adobe XD for prototyping, H5P for interactive content creation; Kahoot! or Quizizz for assessments.
- **Success Criteria:** Microlearning completion rate reaches 90% within 3 months.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overloading modules with information leading to disengagement.
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing

**STEP 4: [Continuous Improvement Loop]**
- **Action:** Implement a feedback loop using pre/post assessments, pulse surveys, and analytics dashboards to measure learning outcomes and course effectiveness.
- **Tools Needed:** LMS analytics dashboard (e.g., Moodle Analytics), Google Forms for pulse surveys.
- **Success Criteria:** Post-training assessment scores improve by 15% within 6 months; engagement metrics meet or exceed targets.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Lack of follow-up on feedback data leading to stagnation in improvements.
- **Time Estimate:** Continuous

**STEP 5: [Optimization & Refinement]**
- **Action:** Regularly update content based on learner performance and industry trends, ensuring relevance and effectiveness.
- **Tools Needed:** Google Drive for version control; LinkedIn Learning or Coursera for upskilling resources.
- **Success Criteria:** Content is updated quarterly with at least 10% new material added each cycle.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Stale content leading to decreased engagement and knowledge retention.
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing
