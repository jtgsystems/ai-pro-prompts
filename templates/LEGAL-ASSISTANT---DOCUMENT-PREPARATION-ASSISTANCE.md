## PHASE 3: EXECUTION WORKFLOW

### Step-by-Step Process
**STEP 1: Document Template Creation**
- **Action:** Draft a template for the target document type using current legal standards.  
- **Tools Needed:** Word/OpenOffice, Notion (free), Google Docs.  
- **Success Criteria:** Template meets all mandatory clauses and complies with relevant laws.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Missing jurisdiction-specific requirements or failing to include required signatures.  
- **Time Estimate:** 2 hours.

**STEP 2: Data Input Setup**
- **Action:** Set up fields for inputs (e.g., client name, case ID) within the template using placeholders.  
- **Tools Needed:** Word/Google Docs with comment features, or Notion database.  
- **Success Criteria:** All required data points are captured and linked to specific document sections.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking mandatory fields leading to incomplete documents.  
- **Time Estimate:** 1 hour.

**STEP 3: AI-Powered Drafting Assistance**
- **Action:** Use an AI legal assistant (e.g., Claude Code with templates) to auto-generate clauses based on inputs.  
- **Tools Needed:** Claude Code (free), ChatGPT with Legal plugin, or premium alternatives like Kira Systems (optional).  
- **Success Criteria:** 95% of drafted sections are error-free and comply with legal standards.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** AI generating incomplete or incorrect clauses due to ambiguous inputs.  
- **Time Estimate:** 1.5 hours.

**STEP 4: Review and Approval Workflow**
- **Action:** Implement a review process where the document is sent for sign-off by relevant parties (e.g., attorney, client).  
- **Tools Needed:** DocuSign (optional), email integration with templates.  
- **Success Criteria:** Document reaches legal approval stage within set deadlines.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Missed approvals leading to delays in service delivery.  
- **Time Estimate:** 2 hours.

**STEP 5: Version Control Implementation**
- **Action:** Set up a system for maintaining version history and tracking changes.  
- **Tools Needed:** Google Drive (free), Notion, or premium alternatives like Confluence.  
- **Success Criteria:** All versions are accessible with clear change logs.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Inconsistent naming conventions leading to confusion over which document is current.  
- **Time Estimate:** 1 hour.

**STEP 6: Client Communication Integration**
- **Action:** Embed client-specific communication templates into the workflow for email or chat notifications.  
- **Tools Needed:** Mailmerge in Gmail (free), or premium alternatives like HubSpot.  
- **Success Criteria:** Clients receive accurate and timely updates about document status.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Incorrect placeholders leading to errors in communications.  
- **Time Estimate:** 1 hour.

**STEP 7: Accessibility Compliance Check**
- **Action:** Run documents through accessibility tools to ensure compliance with ADA standards (e.g., PDFs are tagged correctly).  
- **Tools Needed:** Adobe Acrobat Reader, or free alternatives like LibreOffice.  
- **Success Criteria:** Documents pass automated accessibility tests without errors flagged in required sections.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking font size requirements for screen readers.  
- **Time Estimate:** 30 minutes.

**STEP 8: Secure Storage and Backup**
- **Action:** Archive completed documents in a secure, encrypted location with version history.  
- **Tools Needed:** Google Drive (free), Dropbox Business (optional premium tier).  
- **Success Criteria:** Documents are stored according to compliance standards with backups available for disaster recovery.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Storing unencrypted versions leading to data breaches risk.  
- **Time Estimate:** 1 hour.

**STEP 9: Audit Trail and Reporting**
- **Action:** Log all changes, approvals, and communications related to the document lifecycle.  
- **Tools Needed:** Notion (free), or premium alternatives like ServiceNow.  
- **Success Criteria:** Complete audit trail is available for legal review and compliance checks.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Missing logs leading to gaps in accountability.  
- **Time Estimate:** 1 hour.

**STEP 10: Client Satisfaction Feedback Loop**
- **Action:** After document delivery, collect feedback from the client on clarity, completeness, and timeliness.  
- **Tools Needed:** SurveyMonkey (free tier), or Google Forms.  
- **Success Criteria:** Achieve ≥25% improvement in satisfaction scores compared to previous cycles.  
- **Common Pitfalls:** Lack of follow-up leading to repeated issues.  
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing.
