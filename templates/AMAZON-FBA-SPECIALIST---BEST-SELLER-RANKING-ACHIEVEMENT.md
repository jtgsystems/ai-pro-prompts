### Ultimate Goal
**Primary Objective:** Achieve Best Seller Ranking (BSR) for a product category or specific products on Amazon, maintaining the ranking over time while achieving defined sales targets.

## PHASE 1: INFORMATION GATHERING

### Required Inputs
List what information the agent needs to start:

1. **Input 1:** Product ASIN(s) or SKU list (minimum of 5 products)
   - Format: ASIN, SKU number
   - Validation: Verify each is a valid Amazon product with sales data available

2. **Input 2:** Target category or specific product name(s)
   - Format: Category name or product title
   - Validation: Ensure the target has sufficient search volume and competition

3. **Input 3:** Desired BSR goal (e.g., top 10, top 100)
   - Format: Numeric rank range
   - Validation: Ensure it's achievable based on current metrics

4. **Input 4:** Sales target per month (e.g., $5,000, $20,000)
   - Format: Dollar amount
   - Validation: Realistic given market size and competition

5. **Input 5:** Marketing budget for acquisition campaigns
   - Format: Monthly dollar amount
   - Validation: Should not exceed industry averages

6. **Input 6:** Preferred fulfillment method (e.g., FBA, FBM)
   - Format: String value
   - Validation: Confirm availability and cost structure

   - Deliverable: Detailed ad copy and targeting plan with daily budget allocation

## PHASE 3: EXECUTION WORKFLOW

### Step-by-Step Process

**STEP 1: [Product Selection & Listing Optimization]**
- **Action:** Conduct keyword research, optimize listing elements (title, bullet points, images), finalize pricing strategy
- **Tools Needed:** Helium10 Keyword Explorer, Jungle Scout Keyword Finder, Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator, Canva
- **Success Criteria:** Completed optimized product listings with high-quality images uploaded and pending approval
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking keyword stuffing, neglecting mobile optimization of listing elements
- **Time Estimate:** 4 weeks

**STEP 2: [Initial Advertising Campaign Launch]**
- **Action:** Set up Amazon Sponsored Products campaigns targeting top keywords, allocate initial budget ($500/month), monitor daily performance
- **Tools Needed:** Keepa Price Tracker (free tier), Jungle Scout PPC Calculator, Google Sheets for tracking metrics
- **Success Criteria:** Active campaign with at least 5 targeted ads approved and running
- **Common Pitfalls:** Setting bid too high leading to low CTR, not monitoring conversion rates closely
- **Time Estimate:** 2 weeks

**STEP 3: [Inventory Preparation & FBA Processing]**
- **Action:** Arrange inventory shipment to Amazon fulfillment center, verify packaging quality, complete necessary paperwork
- **Tools Needed:** DHL/UPS shipping software integration with Seller Central, Inventory Management Software (e.g., Sortly)
- **Success Criteria:** All SKUs are stored in the designated Amazon warehouse location, receiving reports uploaded successfully
- **Common Pitfalls:** Incorrect SKU mapping leading to stock discrepancies, not tracking inbound inventory properly
- **Time Estimate:** 1 week

**STEP 4: [Launch & Monitor Initial Sales]**
- **Action:** Promote product through social media and influencer outreach, monitor initial sales performance (first 2 weeks), analyze traffic sources
- **Tools Needed:** Amazon Advertising Console, Google Analytics for referral traffic, Buffer/Hootsuite for scheduling posts
- **Success Criteria:** Achieve target weekly sales volume within first month, positive feedback from early buyers
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overextending marketing budget without monitoring ROI, ignoring negative reviews promptly
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing

**STEP 5: [Optimize Based on Performance Data]**
- **Action:** Adjust bids based on CTR and conversion rates, update product images based on customer feedback, improve listing copy for better relevance
- **Tools Needed:** Amazon Ads Manager, Helium10 Backlink Analyzer, Photoshop for image tweaks
- **Success Criteria:** Achieve sustained increase in BSR and sales over 4 weeks post-launch
- **Common Pitfalls:** Ignoring data-driven insights leading to stagnation, not regularly refreshing content
- **Time Estimate:** Continuous

**STEP 6: [Seasonal/Budget Adjustments]**
- **Action:** Reassess quarterly budget allocations based on performance benchmarks, plan for seasonal inventory boosts if applicable
- **Tools Needed:** Monthly sales reports in Google Sheets, Amazon Forecast tools (free tier)
- **Success Criteria:** Budget allocated aligns with projected sales volumes and marketing needs
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overlooking seasonal trends leading to stockouts or overspending during slower periods
- **Time Estimate:** Quarterly

**STEP 7: [Scaling Campaigns]**
- **Action:** Increase ad spend based on conversion efficiency, expand keyword list for new campaigns, explore additional product variations
- **Tools Needed:** Amazon PPC Manager, Excel/Google Sheets for budget scaling calculations
- **Success Criteria:** Achieve higher sales volume with efficient use of marketing dollars (e.g., lower CPM)
- **Common Pitfalls:** Spreading resources too thin leading to underperformance across campaigns
- **Time Estimate:** 4 weeks per scale phase

**STEP 8: [Competitor Response]**
- **Action:** Monitor competitor listings and ads, adjust strategy to maintain or improve BSR when they launch new promotions
- **Tools Needed:** Jungle Scout Competition Tracker, Keepa Historical Price Charts
- **Success Criteria:** Maintain competitive positioning despite competitors' actions
- **Common Pitfalls:** Ignoring competitor activity leading to gradual loss of market share
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing

**STEP 9: [Customer Experience Enhancement]**
- **Action:** Respond to all reviews (positive and negative), encourage repeat purchases, offer hassle-free returns
- **Tools Needed:** Review management software (e.g., ReviewMeta), email automation for follow-ups
- **Success Criteria:** Increase in positive review ratings and average rating from new reviews
- **Common Pitfalls:** Delayed responses leading to loss of credibility
- **Time Estimate:** Weekly

**STEP 10: [Long-term Optimization]**
- **Action:** Refine ad targeting based on customer behavior, introduce limited-time promotions, explore new marketing channels (e.g., TikTok shopping)
- **Tools Needed:** Amazon Advertising API for automation, TikTok Ads Manager
- **Success Criteria:** Consistent sales growth and improved BSR over 6+ months post-launch
- **Common Pitfalls:** Overreliance on a single channel leading to stagnation when that channel's audience wanes
- **Time Estimate:** Ongoing

## PHASE 4: OPTIMIZATION & REFINEMENT

### Performance Metrics
Define how to measure success:

1. **Primary Metric:** Achieve and maintain desired BSR (e.g., Top 50)
   - Target: Maintain within Top 50 for at least 6 months
   - Measurement Method: Regularly track using Jungle Scout or Helium10 ranking tools

2. **Secondary Metrics:**
   - Sales Volume: Reach target sales/month ($5,000 to $20,000 based on category)
     - Measurement: Amazon Seller Central Reports
   - Conversion Rate: Achieve average conversion rate of 3-5% across ad spend
     - Measurement: Ads Manager Analytics
   - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Maintain CAC below industry average (e.g., <$30 for general retail)
     - Measurement: Total Ad Spend / Units Sold

**Long-term Metrics:**
- Revenue Growth Rate: Achieve 20%+ growth year-over-year
- Repeat Purchase Rate: Aim for 10-15% repeat purchases from existing customers

### Iterative Improvement Loop
1. Measure current performance against targets (using the tools listed in Phase 3)
2. Identify top 3 improvement opportunities based on data trends
3. Implement changes (e.g., adjust bids, refresh listings)
4. Re-measure to verify impact
5. Repeat until ultimate goal of sustained BSR and sales targets is achieved
